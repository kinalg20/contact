How JusBid works?

JusBid provides hotel rooms and MICE services based on the bidding process. If you are a hotelier, you can earn income on your unused inventory, and if you are a customer, you can get hotel rooms at a lower price.

Book a hotel room?

Open the site/app and look for the desired hotel. Make a bid. If you are booking a hotel on an instant basis, the hotel will confirm your booking bid within half an hour. When confirmed, the room is yours for the desired date and time!

Book a hotel room in advance

Now you can book a hotel more than three days before your stay. For that, you have to look for the hotel and place a bid. The maximum period a hotel will take to confirm your bid is two days. When they confirm the bid, you can book the stay well in advance!

What if my bid gets cancelled?

In this bidding system, the hotel can reject your bid if it is not satisfied with the price. In this case, you can place a bid again on a higher price or look for another hotel. We provide plenty of hotel rooms to book!
